# Risk Management Rules

- **Max Position per Symbol:** 2% of total portfolio equity.
- **Max Daily Loss:** 5% of portfolio equity. Stop all trading if exceeded.
- **Stop‑Loss:** Must be set for every trade, at least 1% away from entry.
- **Take‑Profit:** Recommended 2%‑3% above entry.
- **Leverage:** Not used for spot trading.
- **Order Size Rounding:** Round to Binance minimum lot size.
